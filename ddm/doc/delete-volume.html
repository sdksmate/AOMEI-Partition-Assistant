Delete Volume
When the volume is useless you could delete it to release more unallocated space for other volumes use to maximize the use of space.

Note:
  • All of the data on the volume will be deleted when you delete a volume; therefore, we highly recommend backing up all the important data when you want to delete one volume.
  • All of the volume slice will be deleted if you delete one volume.

Delete Volume Step by Step
Step1. Launch Aomei Dynamic Disk Manager. Right-click the volume you want to delete, and select "Delete Volume".

Step2. Click "Ok" in the pop-up window. When you delete one volume, it will be showed as unallocated space.

Step3. Click "Commit" on the toolbar to perform the operation.
