Move Volume Slice Wizard

By using "Move Volume Slice Wizard" of Aomei Dynamic Disk Manager, it could assist you realize move any volume slice from one dynamic disk to any place.

Note:

  1. There must be an unallocated space which is equal to or larger than the volume slice you want to move on the destination place.
  2. After moving, the place of the source volume slice will become as an unallocated space.

Step1. Launch Aomei Dynamic Disk Manager, click "Move Volume Slice Wizard" on the main interface, and click "Next" in the pop-up window.

Step2. Select the volume that you want to move, click "Next", then select the volume slice which you want to move, click "Next".

Step3. Select the destination where you want to move. Click "Finish".

Step4. Click "Commit" on the tool bar to finish the operation.

Tips: To learn more examples please refer to "Aomei Dynamic Disk Manager Case Study".
